Movement Control Rehabilitation

(MOCORE) Laboratory

Nataraj Lab


The MOCORE Laboratory is located at Stevens Institute of Technology in the Department of Biomedical Engineering. The lab investigates solutions to optimize rehabilitation through cognitive agency and multi-sensory feedback. We also offer a community educational program centered around the development of virtual reality (VR) platforms.


We are actively recruiting new members of all academic levels to join the MOCORE lab. Please contact Dr. Nataraj for further information (rnataraj@stevens.edu).
