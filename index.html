Movement Control Rehabilitation

(MOCORE) Laboratory

Nataraj Lab


The MOCORE Laboratory is located at Stevens institute of Technology in the Department of Biomedical Engineering. The lab investigates solutions to optimize rehabilitation through cognitive agency, multi-sensory feedback, and musculoskeletal control simulations.


We are actively recruiting new members of all academic levels to join the MOCORE lab beginning in the Fall 2020 semester. Please contact Dr. Nataraj for further information (rnataraj@stevens.edu).

Lab Mission Statement:

Cognitively Integrating Persons with Movement Disability to Assistive Devices
